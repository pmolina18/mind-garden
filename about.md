# About Mind Garden

Welcome to Mind Garden — a personal blog for thoughts on diverse topics without a rigid thematic structure.

## About the Author

Hi, I'm the author behind Mind Garden. This space serves as a place to explore ideas, share reflections, and document things I find interesting across a variety of subjects.

## About This Blog

Mind Garden is built with Jekyll and hosted on GitHub Pages. The name reflects the idea of cultivating thoughts — planting seeds of ideas and watching them grow over time. Topics range freely, and there's no strict hierarchy; instead, posts are organized through tags and categories for easy discovery.

Feel free to browse around and explore whatever catches your eye.
